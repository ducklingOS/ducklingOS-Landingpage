About Duckling

Duckling is a free, open-source operating system. We started this project out of a passion for technology and a desire to create a great OS that anyone can use, customize, and build upon.

DucklingOS is a work-in-progress based on Linux, designed to be intuitive for casual users and beginners, while remaining powerful and flexible for IT experts and advanced users. We believe in putting users first and creating an OS that works for you, not against you.

While we leverage modern tools, including AI, for development assistance, all core design, programming, and workflow decisions are our own. We started development on June 26th, 2026, and look forward to a successful future for the OS, our team, and our community.

Roadmap

Our vision for the future of DucklingOS. Plans are flexible and will be shaped by the community.

New Major Edition (2029)

A completely new version of the OS, potentially under a new name. Details to be determined as we approach this milestone.

DucklingOS 1.1.x (2026-2027)

Major update with new features, improved hardware support, better performance, and more applications. Detailed plans will be shared closer to the date.

DucklingOS 1.0.1 (Q3 2026)

Security updates, bug fixes, and minor feature additions. First maintenance release after the initial 1.0 launch.

DucklingOS 1.0 (June 2026)

Initial stable release featuring the core operating system with basic applications: File Manager, Terminal, Settings, and Web Browser.
