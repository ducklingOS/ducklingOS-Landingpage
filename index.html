About Duckling

Duckling is a free Open Source operating system. We started building because of bordem and we want to achieve a good operating system everyone can use in their own way and can reuse as a base for their own systems.

DucklingOS is a work in progress operating system based on Linux, supposed to be made easy to use for casual PC users or beginners but powerful and good for PC / IT experts or advanced users we are developing it with AI parts (as a disclaimer) we do program and work on it on our own and all design and possible work flow is done by us. We are working on it since the 26th of June 2026, and are looking forward for a hopeful and hopefully a sucessful future for the OS, us and our users.
